Frequently Asked Questions
Can I avoid being asked to trust the software every time I use it?
PC: In the security warning dialog, check the box marked "Always trust content from this publisher".
Mac: In the dialogue asking you to trust the software, check the box marked "Allow all applets from 'site' with this signature".

Who produced the BSL Examples?
The examples in the site were produced by Theodore Jones, City of Norwich School, during his Year 10 work experience placement at UEA in July 2010.
The signs were developed using HamNoSys, the Hamburg Notation System, and animated using software developed at UEA.

Where does the signing avatar software come from?
The software was developed in the School of Computing Sciences at UEA.
For more information on avatar signing see the UEA Virtual Humans Group web site.
